# Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** [Current Date] **Last Updated:** [Current Date] ## Our Commitment to Your Privacy At PromoFlow, we believe developers should focus on building great apps, not worrying about data privacy. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. **Bottom line:** We collect the minimum data needed to distribute your promo codes effectively. We don't sell your data, we don't track users across the web, and we delete inactive accounts automatically. ## What Information We Collect ### Developer Account Information - **Email address** - Required for account creation and notifications - **App/project details** - App name, platform, description (for organizing your campaigns) - **Usage statistics** - Number of codes uploaded, distributed, and redeemed (for improving our service) ### Promo Code Data - **Code values** - The actual promo codes you upload - **Platform targeting** - Which platforms each code works on (iOS, Android, etc.) - **Usage status** - Whether codes have been claimed or used - **Distribution timestamps** - When codes were claimed by users ### End User Data (Minimal) - **Platform detection** - Basic device/browser info to serve the right code - **IP address** - Temporarily stored for fraud prevention (deleted after 30 days) - **Redemption confirmation** - Whether the code worked (anonymous aggregate data only) ### What We Don't Collect - No personal information from end users - No tracking cookies or persistent identifiers - No browsing history or behavioral data - No social media profiles or third-party account data ## How We Use Your Information ### Core Service Operations - **Code distribution** - Matching users to the right codes for their platform - **Real-time tracking** - Showing you which codes have been used vs. unused - **Fraud prevention** - Detecting suspicious patterns in code redemption - **Service notifications** - Alerting you when code pools are running low ### Service Improvements - **Performance optimization** - Analyzing response times and error rates - **Feature development** - Understanding which features developers use most - **Bug fixes** - Identifying and resolving technical issues ### We Never Use Data For - Advertising or marketing to end users - Selling to third parties - Building user profiles - Cross-platform tracking ## Data Security & Protection ### Technical Safeguards - **Encryption in transit** - All data encrypted using TLS 1.3 - **Encryption at rest** - Database and file storage encrypted with AES-256 - **Access controls** - Role-based permissions with multi-factor authentication - **Regular backups** - Encrypted backups stored in geographically separate locations ### Infrastructure Security - **Cloud provider:** AWS (SOC 2 Type II certified) - **Database security:** Automated patching and security updates - **Network security:** Web Application Firewall (WAF) and DDoS protection - **Monitoring:** 24/7 automated threat detection and logging ### Data Minimization - **Automatic deletion:** Inactive developer accounts deleted after 12 months - **Temporary storage:** End user IP addresses deleted after 30 days - **Code cleanup:** Expired codes automatically purged after 90 days - **Log retention:** System logs kept for 6 months maximum ## Third-Party Services We work with a minimal set of trusted partners: ### Essential Services - **AWS** - Cloud hosting and database services - **Stripe** - Payment processing (if/when we add paid features) - **SendGrid** - Transactional emails (account notifications only) ### Analytics (Privacy-Focused) - **Plausible Analytics** - Privacy-friendly website analytics (no personal data) - No Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other tracking services ### What We Share - **Nothing personal** - We never share developer or user personal information - **Aggregate statistics only** - Anonymous usage patterns for service improvement - **Legal compliance** - Only if required by valid legal process ## Your Rights & Controls ### Developer Account Management - **Data access** - Download all your data in JSON format anytime - **Account deletion** - Delete your account and all associated data instantly - **Data correction** - Update your information through account settings - **Export codes** - Download your promo code lists as CSV files ### Privacy Controls - **Email preferences** - Control which notifications you receive - **Data retention** - Set custom deletion schedules for completed campaigns - **Visibility settings** - Control whether your campaigns appear in public usage stats ### Exercising Your Rights Send requests to privacy@promoflow.io with your account email. We respond within 48 hours and fulfill most requests within 7 days. ## Data Retention & Deletion ### Active Campaigns - Code data retained as long as campaigns are active - Usage statistics kept for ongoing tracking and analytics - Account information maintained while account is active ### Completed Campaigns - Detailed logs kept for 90 days after campaign completion - Summary statistics retained for 1 year (for your reference) - All data permanently deleted after retention period ### Account Deletion When you delete your account: - **Immediate:** Account access revoked, personal info deleted - **Within 24 hours:** All promo codes and campaigns deleted - **Within 7 days:** All backups and cached data purged ## International Data Transfers ### Data Location - **Primary storage:** United States (AWS US-East-1) - **Backup storage:** Ireland (AWS EU-West-1) - **Processing:** All data processing occurs in the US or EU ### Transfer Safeguards - **Standard Contractual Clauses** - EU-approved data transfer mechanisms - **Adequate security** - All locations maintain equivalent security standards - **Limited transfers** - Data only transferred for backup and redundancy ## Children's Privacy PromoFlow is designed for developers and businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you're under 18, please have a parent or guardian create and manage the account. ## Changes to This Policy We'll notify you of material changes via email and prominently on our website.
