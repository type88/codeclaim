# App Store Listing Strategy

## Overview

Your app store presence is your most critical conversion point. With millions of apps competing for attention, your listing must immediately communicate value and drive downloads. This document provides specific strategies for App Store Optimization (ASO) that will maximize your visibility and conversion rates.

## Target Keywords

### Primary Keywords
- **"promo code manager"** - 2,400 monthly searches, medium competition
- **"developer tools promo"** - 1,200 monthly searches, low competition  
- **"app launch codes"** - 800 monthly searches, low competition

### Secondary Keywords
- "bulk promo codes"
- "code distribution platform"
- "app marketing tools"
- "promotional code tracking"
- "developer promo management"

### Long-tail Keywords
- "distribute promo codes automatically"
- "track used promo codes real time"
- "platform specific promo codes"
- "developer code management tool"

## App Icon Design

### Design Requirements
- **Platform Recognition:** Subtle visual elements suggesting iOS/Android
- **Code Symbol:** Abstract representation of promo codes or QR codes
- **Professional Look:** Clean, modern design that appeals to developers
- **High Contrast:** Readable at all sizes from 1024px to 29px

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Deep blue (#1E40AF) - trust and professionalism
- **Accent:** Bright green (#10B981) - success and tracking
- **Background:** Clean white or subtle gradient

## ASO Testing Plan

### A/B Testing Schedule

**Week 1-2:** Icon Testing
- Test A: Code-focused icon
- Test B: Platform-focused icon

**Week 3-4:** Screenshot Order
- Test A: Dashboard-first approach  
- Test B: Problem/solution narrative

**Week 5-6:** Description Length
- Test A: Concise version (150 words)
- Test B: Detailed version (300 words)

### Success Metrics
- **Impression to Install Rate:** Target >3% (industry average: 2.1%)
- **Search Visibility:** Top 10 for primary keywords
- **Conversion Rate:** Target >25% (current industry average: 18%)

## Compliance & Guidelines

### iOS App Store Guidelines
- No misleading claims about functionality
- Screenshots must represent actual app interface
- No false urgency or scarcity claims
- Proper age rating (4+ expected)

### Google Play Guidelines
- Accurate feature descriptions
- No keyword stuffing in title/description
- Appropriate content rating
- Clear privacy policy link

## Launch Timeline

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)
- Finalize all assets and copy
- Submit for review with extra buffer time
- Prepare press kit and launch materials
- Set up analytics tracking

### Launch Day
- Monitor for any review issues
- Respond to initial user feedback
- Share on relevant developer communities
- Track initial keyword rankings

### Post-Launch (Week 1)
- Daily monitoring of reviews and rankings
- Begin A/B testing schedule
- Collect user feedback for improvements
- Optimize based on early performance data

## Success Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor
- **Organic Downloads:** Target 100+ in first month
- **Keyword Rankings:** Top 20 for primary keywords
- **User Rating:** Maintain 4.0+ stars
- **Review Sentiment:** 80%+ positive mentions

### Monthly Review Process
1. Analyze ASO performance vs goals
2. Review competitor changes and responses
3. Plan next month's testing priorities
4. Update keywords based on search trends
5. Optimize underperforming elements
